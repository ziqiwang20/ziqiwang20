# Hi, I'm Ziqi (Eddie) Wang 👋

I am a PhD candidate in the School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University, supervised by Prof. Ibrahim Khalil, Dr. Xiaoyu Xia, and Dr. Jason Xue. My research focuses on secure and efficient agentic systems, with interests in multi-agent intelligence, parallel and distributed learning, and privacy-preserving computing.

I am currently working on resource-efficient multi-agent LLM systems, federated foundation model fine-tuning, and privacy-aware edge computing, aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical foundations and real-world AI service deployment.
